Privacy Policy

Beyond Scan believes in privacy and online freedom and that it should be very clear how we protect and manage your personal information. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us via zzsoftservice@outlook.com.

Beyond Scan is a no-log iOS application. Except for the limited exceptions below, we do not collect any information transmitted through the network. More specifically, we do not collect any data stored on or transmitted from your device, including any data that applications on your device may transmit through our network. 

- How we manage your personal information

We automatically collect certain non-Personal Information from Users. For instance, Beyond Scan assigns a unique identifier to each User of the Service, but Beyond Scan does not tie these unique identifiers to the Personal Information of Users.

- How do we deal with your private information?

Beyond Scan will only collect non-personal information needed for it to properly conduct its business and only collect it by fair and lawful means.

[ - ]To improve customer service

When we have any problems in specific areas, we will use Email to communicate in order to get feedback and best suggestions. With fluent communication, it is helpful to get better customer support with you.
